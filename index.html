Data collection

We collect no personal data. Our apps do not require an internet connection and do not have network access permission. No information — including your name, email address, IP address, device identifiers, location, or usage statistics — is ever transmitted to us or any third party.

Local storage

All data you enter into our apps (measurements, records, notes, schedules, logs, characters, etc.) is stored exclusively on your device using local device storage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to Calpix Studio or anyone else.

Third-party services

Our apps do not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising networks, crash reporting services, or SDKs that collect data. There are no ads in any of our apps.

Children

Because our apps collect no data at all, there is no risk to users of any age. We do not knowingly solicit or collect information from children under 13. Parents and guardians can allow children to use our apps with confidence that no personal information is captured.

Data deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete it at any time by clearing the app's data in your device settings or by uninstalling the app. There is nothing stored on our end to delete.

Security

Data stored by our apps resides in your device's sandboxed local storage, protected by Android's and iOS's built-in security model. We have no servers and therefore no server-side security risks.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy if our practices change. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Since we collect no data, any changes are unlikely to affect your privacy in any meaningful way.
